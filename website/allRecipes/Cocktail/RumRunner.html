Rum Runner

Ingredients

  • 1 Ounce Light Rum
  • 1 Ounce Dark Rum
  • 1 Ounce Banana Liqueur
  • 1 Ounce Orange Juice
  • 1 Ounce Pineapple Juice
  • 1/2 Ounce Cranberry Juice

Directions

  1. Combine ingredients and serve over crushed ice.

Glass

Notes

Spiced rum and Malibu can be added or substituted. A splash of grenadine can also be added for extra sweetness.
