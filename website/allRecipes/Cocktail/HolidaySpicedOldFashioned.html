Holiday Spiced Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 4 Ounces Barrel Proof Rye Whiskey
  • 4 Ounces Demerara Rum
  • 1 Ounce Allspice Dram
  • 1 Ounce Pure Maple Syrup or Chai Tea Syrup
  • 8 Dashes Angostura Bitters
  • 1 Pinch Salt (Very Small Pinch)

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a jar or pitcher and shake to mix.
  2. Serve over an ice cube and garnish with an orange twist.
