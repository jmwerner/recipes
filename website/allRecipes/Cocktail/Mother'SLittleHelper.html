Mother's Little Helper

Recipe from Dan Wallien

Ingredients

  • 1 Ounce Peaches, Sliced
  • 1 Ounce Peach Schnapps
  • 3 Ounces Scotch
  • 6 Ounces Iced Tea
  • 1 Pinch Fresh Mint
  • 1 Pinch Lemon

Directions

  1. Combine peaches, peach schnapps, scotch, and iced tea.
  2. Pour mixture over mint and lemon. Enjoy on ice.
